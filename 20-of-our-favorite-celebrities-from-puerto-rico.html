20 of Our Favorite Celebrities from Puerto Rico

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, July 26, 2024

Puerto Rico is a beautiful place with a rich culture and history. It’s no surprise that it has produced so many talented celebrities. Puerto Rico is well known for its music and athletes, but it has also produced some very successful actors and actresses. Some of them started from very humble beginnings and have become very successful overtime. That is why they have become an icon and they encourage others to work hard everyday. Here are 20 of our favorite celebrities from Puerto Rico

20. Benicio Del Toro (Actor and Producer)

Benicio Del Toro is a Puerto Rican actor and producer who has won an Academy Award, Golden Globe, and Screen Actors Guild Award. 19. Roberto Clemente (Baseball player)

Roberto Clemente is considered one of the greatest baseball players of all time. He was a right fielder for the Pittsburgh Pirates and played in 18 All-Star Games.
