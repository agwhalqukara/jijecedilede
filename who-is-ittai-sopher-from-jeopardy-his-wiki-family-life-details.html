As per wiki, he is a native of Englewood, New Jersey, United States. He was raised in a Jewish family and his parents are Jonathan Sopher and Avira Sopher. His parents have been married since 6 September 1993.

Ittai is accompanied by three brothers named Ehud  Sopher, Matan McGiver Johnson Sopher, and Levi Sopher. Ehud is a freelance director and videographer who graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Film and Television from New York University.

Ittai Sopher poses in a family picture with his parents and brothers. (Source: Avira’s Facebook)

A Jeopardy fan, he has been watching the show as a kid in 2010. He is a fan of the host Ken Jennings who has 74 consecutive wins on the show. Moreover, he said that he would like to use the winning money to save for the future and also do some traveling. 

His Education Details

Sopher pursued his high school education at SAR High School in New York City, New York. After graduating in 2015, he went to Pitzer College and earned a Bachelor of Arts in Political Studies in 2019.

During college, he was an active member of the Pitzer community and served as a Resident Assistant, Advancement Ambassador, New Student Mentor, and Orientation Leader at the Office of Student Affairs.

Ittai also participated in a semester-long exchange program with La Universidad de San Francisco de Quito, a university on the outskirts of Quito, Ecuador in 2017. Moreover, he earned a master’s degree in Journalism from the University of Arizona in the year 2023.

What Is Ittai Sopher’s Job?

Sopher holds the job of a digital journalist and news producer. As per his Linkedin, he has reported, produced, and worked in production on all major mediums: television, radio, podcast, print, and social media. 

Currently, he serves as a news producer at WWL-TV, a CBS-affiliated television station in New Orleans, Louisiana, United States. Prior to that, he served as a production assistant and field technician at Arizona Public Media.

Ittai worked in news production in Washington, D.C. with veteran anchor Greta Van Susteren at Voice of America, for the special projects team at ABC7 in Los Angeles. He was a News Team Staff Researcher at Ballotpedia, serving for several months.

Furthermore, he has a brief experience working as an administrative assistant at the Commonwealth Business Bank and as a podcast producer for The Student Life Newspaper. He was a staff writer at the Claremont Journal of Law and Public Policy.

Is Ittai Sopher Married Or Dating?

Sopher is unmarried and has not disclosed his relationship status in public. Therefore, not much is known about his love life.
