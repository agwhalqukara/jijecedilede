Real Housewives of Atlanta Reunion FIght: Porsha Attacks Kenya

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, September 27, 2024

The hype was real! Kenya Moore and Porsha Williams really, truly got into it during part one of the Real Housewives of Atlanta reunion — with the much-promised brawl going down after the two women traded barbs back and forth relentlessly. Former beauty queen Moore, 43, wielded a scepter and, later, a megaphone as she leveled her attacks against newly divorced Williams, 31.

The arch-nemeses goaded one another with one insult after another. “There is no African prince for you,” Williams told her. “You just recently tried to pay someone to be on the show. . . You have been a villain from day one.”

“Gone with the Wind Fabulous” singer Moore then claimed the Williams’ ex-husband Kordell Stewart cheated on her — which, it turns out, was the straw that broke the camel’s back. “You’re a slut from the the ’90s!” Williams screamed.

Using the megaphone, Moore retorted: “You are a dumb ho, shut up!”
