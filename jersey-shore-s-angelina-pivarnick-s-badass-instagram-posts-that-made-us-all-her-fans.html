Fans should be aware that MTV’s Angelina Pivarnick is “often imitated, never duplicated.” Prior to the Jersey Shore: Family Vacation Season 5B release, the reality TV personality used her Instagram Stories to “vent” to followers before going inside a grocery shop. Find out what she had to say and when Jersey Shore: Family Vacation viewers may expect to see new episodes.

Angelina Pivarnick, the star of “Jersey Shore: Family Vacation,” uploaded a selfie to her Instagram Stories in May 2022.

Angelina Pivarnick’s Instagram Stories show her ‘venting’ on May 16

Yesterday, the reality star went grocery shopping but first paused to upload a video on her Instagram Stories. Angelina said, “I guess I’m going to start acting like a barbarian. “I think I need to accomplish that in life,” she replied.

So from this point on, Angelina is a f***ing beast, she continued. It’s a movement, so get ready, people. It’s taking place, so let’s f***ing do it.

Uncertain of why, Angelina stated in the following slide that she wasn’t “happy.” “Pissed today is an understatement,” she added.

The last line of Angelina’s video was another one about “running rings around young motherf***ers.” She uttered:

No of my age, I can still have a good time. I demonstrate this every weekend. People are frequently copied but never exactly duplicated. I’m f***ing just saying. I’m going to say that right now. Run rings around motherf***ers who are 21 years old. Let’s go, any f***ing day.”

‘Jersey Shore’ star Angelina Pivarnick says there’s ‘more to come’

The first half of Jersey Shore: Family Vacation was already halfway through when the divorce of Chris Larangeira and Angelina was announced. As seen in season 3 of Jersey Shore: Family Vacation, the pair wed in November 2019. In January 2021, Angelina first filed for divorce, but she never served Chris with the documents. Chris submitted a formal claim in January 2022.

While they were still living apart, the second half of the season was being filmed. Since then, Angelina has teased that in upcoming episodes, viewers will learn the truth. Angelina concluded her Instagram Stories post by promising “more to come.” A couple of days ago, she tweeted:

It will literally be insane in @JerseyShore’s upcoming season! Guys, stay tuned. I’m serious when I say this.

SallyAnn Salsano, a Jersey Shore producer, also defended Angelina in a different tweet. In a retweet, she stated, “I can confirm.”

‘Jersey Shore: Family Vacation’ will likely release new episodes soon

As of the time of publication, MTV had not specified a Jersey Shore: Family Vacation release date. The ensemble, however, has already gone on their “family vacations,” which this time included visits to San Diego and El Paso, Texas.
