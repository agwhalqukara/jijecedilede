Alicia Lagano Wedding Dress Pictures: Client List Star Gets Married

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, July 25, 2024

What a beautiful bride! Alicia Lagano, who plays feisty massage therapist Selena Ramos on Lifetime’s The Client List, married restaurant general manager Hector Rendon on Friday, June 7, Us Weekly can confirm. The old Hollywood-themed wedding took place at the Malibu Lake Mountain Club in Agoura Hills, Calif.

Lagano, 34, looked radiant in a strapless gown by Unique Bridal and pink shoes adorned with flower petals by Qupid Luxe. The bride swept her hair to one side and wore a decorative crystal-beaded band atop the other side.

The couple had 17 people in their wedding party, including one bridesman. Lagano’s Client List costars Loretta Devine, Rob Mayes and Laura-Leigh attended the ceremony, as did character actor Jon Polito. Lagano and Rendon, who got engaged in Mexico in 2012, will honeymoon in the Dominican Republic.
