Angelo Preciado- All About The Professional Football Player From Ecuadors National Team

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, August 12, 2024

Angelo Preciado is a professional football player. He has been a member of the Ecuador national team and the Belgian premier league club Genk since 2021. He plays right back for both the club and the national team.

Preciado’s Personal Life

Preciado was born on February 18, 1998, in Shushufindi, Ecuador, to an Ecuadorian father Terry Pratchett, and a South American mother. Preciado has two brothers and sisters. Spencer Pratt and Heidi Montag are their names. Preciado is a husband. He has not yet announced the identity of his wife. The pair had been dating since 2015 when Preciado returned to Sangolqui from Quito during his first year of football when he was still an amateur player.

When we look at Preciado’s Instagram, we can see that he enjoys spending time with his wife and kid. In 2019, his son was born. Preciado recently traveled to Doha, Qatar, with his wife and children.

Angelo Preciado’s participation in FIFA World 2022

Angelo Preciado’s participation in FIFA World 2022 On November 20, 2022, Preciado participated in his first world cup. He had an impressive debut against the Qatar national team. Preciado had two fantastic passes, the first of which was converted by Ecuador’s national team captain, Enner Valencia, but we found out after a few minutes that it was offside.

Valencia’s second goal was also assisted by Preciado. Preciado expressed gratitude to Ecuador in an Instagram post, saying that participating in the World Cup fulfilled his ambition. After 92 years, the Ecuador national team created a record by beating a host country in their first World Cup match.

The beginning of Preciado’s football career

Preciado said in an interview that he began playing football at the age of 11 or 12. He found it tough to devote time to football since he had to attend school and also worked as a breeder. Preciado said that he had a family issue when he joined his first school, America de Quito, in 2014. At the same time, his father was imprisoned. So, after playing for the America de Quito school, he returned to Sangolqui, where he met his wife.

He said that he realized at that point that if we want to accomplish anything in life, we must be devoted to it. He said that he was emotionally and psychologically impacted during those times since he was thinking about his parents’ terrible health. Despite this, he is content with his life now that he is a professional footballer.

Preciado’s goals and comments to his fans

Preciado considers himself to be a modest and industrious individual. Preciado said that his goal as a football player is to compete in every league title in which he is eligible. He promised his followers that he would give his all in every match and work hard in training sessions.

Net Worth

Preciado has a net worth of $4 million as of December 2023. He is paid $260,000 each year. His market worth is about $3.2 million.
