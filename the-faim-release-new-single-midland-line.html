The Faim Release New Single &quot;Midland Line&quot;

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, September 9, 2024

Following their debut release of “Saints Of The Sinners,” Australian group The Faim has shared a second single, titled, “Midland Line,” with producer John Feldmann once again behind the track.

Listen to The Faim’s new single, “Midland Line,” below.

In an exclusive for CelebMix, Stephen Beerkens (bass, keyboard), said that working with John Feldmann on these songs has been “incredible” and that he has been pushing the members to give it their best in each track. Beerkens added that Feldmann “helped us to go from writing two half songs a week back in Perth, to two full songs a day in the studio.”

The Faim, made of Josh Raven, Michael Bono, Stephen Beerkens and Sean Tighe, recently signed to BMG Worldwide and premiered their first single, “Saints Of The Sinners,” last month on the Radio 1 Rock Show’s ‘Rockest Record.’

The guys are set to hit the road next week for shows in the UK with Lower Than Atlantis before heading home to Australia and New Zealand for a leg of shows with Sleeping With Sirens.

The group will also share the stage with PVMNTS for a quick run across the UK in May, as well as headlining their first shows at The Forum in Tunbridge Wells and Stereo in Glasgow and performing at The Great Escape, Slam Dunk, Download, and Sound City Festivals later this summer.
