Know About Trey Flowers; Stats, Patriots, Salary, Wife, Age, Children

At the end of the defensive position, Trey Flowers is tough as they come. The young NFL star, age 25 has put some tremendous numbers with one team in particular.

American Footballer Trey Flowers plays for NFL team New England Patriots. He backs the squad along with his teammates at the defensive end from his splendid stats for the past four years.

Trey Flowers Bio & Family

Born as Robert Lee Flowers III on the 16 August 1993, he was raised in Huntsville, Alabama to his parents, Jacqueline and Robert Flowers. Trey is the seventh child in the family of his ten siblings which includes some of his half brothers and sisters. His sister’s names are Jazzmine, Summer, Jacqueise, and Shanequa and brothers are Rod, Jamal, Ced, Josh, and Joshua.

Trey went to Columbia High School to play basketball and football. Next, he attended the Georgia Institute of Technology to play football but chose to go to the University of Arkansas to play football events. He stands at the height of 6 feet 2 inches (1.87 m) and weighs around 120 kg.

Career & Net Worth

The defensive back earns his prominent net worth from professional NFL career since 2015. However, before playing with New England Patriots, Trey played college football at Arkansas. His impressive stats in the team record 18 sacks and 47.5 tackles for loss, forced four fumbles including broke up 13 passes.

In 2015, the Patriots picked him in the 101st overall in the fourth round of the draft. His big break came in 2016 after gaining first double sacks in week eight against Buffalo Bills. Likewise, he assisted the Patriots to win against Atlanta Falcons with 2.5 sacks.

Another year came with a big surprise when he had 2.0 sacks against the Kansas City Chiefs in 2017. Just like his magnificent stats, Trey’s fortunes are sky high. According to Forbes, he got a salary of $1.907 million by Patriots in the 2018 season.

Does Trey Flowers Have A Wife?

The Patriots star does not have a wife since he has never walked down the aisle. Nevertheless, he cheers a happy relationship with his longtime girlfriend, Tyler Rae Smith. Together, the duo shares two children; a daughter named Skylar Amour and a son, Shylo Reign.
