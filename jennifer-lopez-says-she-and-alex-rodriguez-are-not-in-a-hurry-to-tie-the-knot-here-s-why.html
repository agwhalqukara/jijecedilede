Jennifer Lopez Says She And Alex Rodriguez Are Not In A Hurry To Tie The Knot Heres Why!

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, September 25, 2024

During a new interview with Oprah Winfrey, Jennifer Lopez got candid about a lot of personal aspects of her life as well as her career. One of the topics the triple threat opened up about was her romance with Alex Rodriguez, revealing that she is not in a hurry to tie the knot and the reason why that is!

As fans know, the two are engaged but it turns out that a date for the wedding is yet to be set.

The man popped the big question in March of last year and their relationship is stronger than ever.

J.Lo. joined the famous TV personality on stage at her Los Angeles stop for Oprah’s 2020 Vision: Your Life in Focus tour.

That is when Jen shared her perception of life, saying that she still has a lot more to do and give the world.

‘It is so funny as when we got engaged I was like, ‘Oooh, we’re going to get married in a couple of months?!’ Your old thinking comes back, all the hopeless romantic [thoughts] that made me get married 3 times. He was like, ‘Whatever you want to do, we can talk about it’. I said, ‘But if we are going to be together for the rest of our lives, what’s the rush?’’ she shared with the beloved talk show host.

She went on to dish that ‘We’re really going to try and build something together that we both have never had or never felt like we had – which was a family with a husband and wife, and a mother and father – and we embrace all our children and show them something we didn’t have. It was different than anything I had ever experienced in the sense of his consistency. What he says, he does … every time. And that is big.’
