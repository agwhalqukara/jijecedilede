Is President Donald Trump Creating A Political Dynasty? Donald Trump Jr. Not Ruling Out Presidential

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, August 18, 2024

A new interview with Donald Trump Jr., has some asking if he will one day run for president and if so, is Donald Trump creating a political dynasty. President Donald Trump stunned and shocked the world by becoming president. While there are many who want to see President Trump impeached, there are others who want to see him reelected. Since Donald Trump Jr., publicly spoke about a possible run in the future, there are already people expressing the sentiment that they want President Trump elected for a second term and Donald Trump Jr., to win in 2024.

One social media was so excited at the thought of a Trump political dynasty, he mapped out the future presidency until 2044.

President Donald Trump has been married three times and has children with each of his wives. He was married to Ivana Trump from 1977 until 1992. They had Donald Trump Jr., Eric, and Ivanka Trump. Each of the three has been involved with their father’s 2016 presidential campaign. Ivanka Trump is now an official member of the White House as she and her husband Jared Kushner worked on the campaign and are now advisors to the President. Jared Kushner is President Donald Trump’s senior advisor.

After President Trump’s divorce with Ivana Trump, he married Marla Maples. The couple was married from 1993 until 1999 and the couple had daughter Tiffany Trump in 1993. Though she has made public appearances in support of her father’s presidential run, Tiffany does not work for the president in an official position.

Melania Trump and President Donald Trump were married in 2005 and are the parents to Barron Trump who turned 13 on March 20.
