#BringBack Alice Season 1: Release Date, Cast, Plot, Storyline And Other Updates

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, September 15, 2024

Bring Back Alice is an upcoming HBO series set to premiere in April. Created by Marcin Kubawski and Milosz Sakowski, the series is a Polish show that will follow the life of an influencer gone missing. Kubawski, the series creator, has also worked in several shows, including Love Express, Pakt, The Disappearances of  Wallerian Borowczyk and many others. The show will solve the mystery of the popular influencer who goes missing only to return with amnesia. But does she not remember anything from the past, or is that just a front? Was the escape planned, or is she innocent? Does she have anything to do with the other girl’s disappearance? Only one way to find out. Here is everything we know about the upcoming show.

#BringBack Alice Season 1 Release Date: When will it air?

The show is slated for an April  14th release date. We are yet to be updated on whether the episodes will release on the same day or if they will drop weekly until the last episode. The series will be available for streaming On HBO, and you must get a subscription to binge on the series. It only costs $ 15.99 a month, and you will have access to a  wide array of tv shows, from dramas to documentaries.

#BringBack Alice Season 1 Plot: What will Happen?

Alicja Stec is a famous influencer known for her flashy lifestyle and being the life of the party. Mysteriously, she disappears, and nobody knows her whereabouts. Search parties search for her tirelessly without avail, and soon enough, people assume that she died. However, after not so long, a teenager is found who resembles Stec, but unfortunately, she remembers nothing of her past. Nobody believes she lost her memory; to make matters worse, another teenager also disappeared on the same day. Tomek, the missing woman’s brother, believes the internet idol could be the key to finding Wera, his long-lost sister. But how do they find her when Stec remembers nothing of her past?

#BringBack Alice Season 1 Cast: Who will be in?

The cast lineup of the HBO series has already been released and will feature some of the talented actors and actresses you may have seen in your favourite tv shows. Helena Englert stars as Alicja Stec, the series protagonist. The actress has featured in other shows, including The Closed Circuit, where she starred as Kasia and Pokusa as Inez. She has also been featured in Television Theatre, Signs, Better Half for MY Mother, Diagnoza, The Elements of Sasza and many others. She will be starring alongside Sandra Drzymalska, Howita Budnik, Sebastian Dela, Stanislaw Linowski,  Marieta Zukowska, Marci Stec, Mila Jankowska, Marcel Opalisnki, Katarzyna Galazka, Vitalik Havryla, Adam Cywka, Bartlomiej Deklewa, and Natalia Iwanska.

#BringBack Alice Season 1 Trailer: When can we watch it?

A trailer for the thriller is available, and you can check it out on either YouTube or HBO. The trailer starts with the camera snapping, people murmuring, and a battered woman walking out from what seems like a tunnel. The trailer then progresses to people-watching, liquor and dancing. Soon enough, screams are heard, people die while others have fun, others make out while others grieve for their loved ones. Ensure you check out the series trailer on YouTube for a more comprehensive view of what to expect. Mark your calendars for April 15th, the series debut on HBO.
