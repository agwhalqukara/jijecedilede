Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, August 18, 2024
Growing in Grace Curriculum: God Loves the World: Preschool

"God Loves the World," based on John 3:16, invites children to explore how God expresses love to us and how we should respond. God's love embraces all people in all places, and we find fulfillment in loving all that God loves. Fun songs and activities from around the world bring creativity to your children's ministry. General and seasonal songs are included.

The full year includes two 15-week units, Fall and Spring, for Preschool/Kindergarten children (ages 3-5). Each semester includes fun, creative Songs and Activities, Hymns, Demonstration and Accompaniment Recordings, KIDPages, Song Teaching Steps, Activity Teaching Steps, Colorful Visuals, and Session Outlines.

Due to the digital nature of this product, returns will only be accepted with shrink wrap packaging intact.
