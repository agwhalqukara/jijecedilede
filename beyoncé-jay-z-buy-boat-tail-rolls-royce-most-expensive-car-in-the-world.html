Beyonc &amp; Jay Z Buy Boat Tail Rolls Royce, Most Expensive Car in the World

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 10, 2024

- American superstars Beyoncé and Jay-Z have just bought the most expensive car in the world, the Boat Tail edition of the Rolls Royce

- Since the couple love all things luxury, the car is perfect for them and they can surely afford it with their billionaire status

- Rolls Royce took four years to develop the Boat Tail and it is an ultra-luxurious vehicle which is handcrafted by the company in a limited batch

Beyoncé and Jay-Z, according to rumours, have ordered the first Rolls-Royce Boat Tail. The couple is said to have paid $28 million (R385 million) for the first Boat Tail unit.

Boat Tail, termed the pinnacle of luxury and customisation by Rolls-Royce, was just unveiled. The ultra-luxurious vehicle was handcrafted by the company's Coachbuilding section, which specialises in hand-building cars and setting a new standard for personalisation.

The division took four years to build the Boat Tail according to the demands of the customer, who paid $28 million for the first unit. This makes it the most costly car on the planet, according to a report by TimesNowNews.

The vehicle is perfect for Jay Z and Beyoncé. Since their daughter Blue Ivy was born, they've been passionate about boats, enjoy champagne and have an unabashed passion for all things blue.

“It's thought this fantastic car has been commissioned by Beyoncé and Jay Z, with all its detailing matching their favourite things,” a source told The Telegraph.

Most importantly, with a combined nett worth of $1.9 billion (R26 billion), Jay and Bey can afford to spend a fortune on a bespoke car they'll probably just use once for a picnic.

Briefly News previously reported that Jay-Z explained that he learned to swim after their daughter Blue Ivy was born. Jay-Z is such a protective father and will do anything to shield his daughter from harm.

The US billionaire has explained that he only learned to swim after the birth of his daughter, Blue Ivy. The rapper was speaking candidly during an interview with HBO's The Shop: Uninterrupted.

"If she ever fell in the water and I couldn't get her, I couldn't even fathom that thought. I gotta learn how to swim."

Blue Ivy is the first of three kids Hov shares with his wife, Beyoncé. The award-winning Blue is nine years old now.
