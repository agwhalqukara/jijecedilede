Career

She has taken part in pageants, modelling, and TV, among others.

Pageant career

She was a 22-year-old student when she walked off with the Miss South Africa 2011 title after competing against 11 finalists. She received the crown from 2010's Miss S.A Bokang Motjane. This was the moment that thrust her into the limelight. She previously won the Miss Tropika and Miss Waterkloof titles.

Following the win, she went to compete in Las Vegas in Miss Universe 2012 and ended up placing 6th in the competition.

Career scandals

In 2012, she withdrew from the Miss World Competition, and her replacement was the first princess Remona Moodley.

Withdrawing from such a career-defining competition led to tons of speculation at the time. Some claimed she was pregnant or sick. There was even a rumour that she was scheduled to get her breasts enlarged.

She rose above the rumours and worked hard to build her charity project so the false stories would not negatively impact her career.
