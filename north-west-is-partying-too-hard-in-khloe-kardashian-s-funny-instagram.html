North West Is Partying Too Hard in Khloe Kardashian's Funny Instagram

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, July 6, 2024

Don’t lose your chill, Nori! For those who don’t follow the Kardashians’ every move (do those people exist?!), the famed reality TV family is currently vacationing in St. Barts. Naturally, that means there have been a slew of fun family Instagram shots from the post French Caribbean island. 

On Monday, Aug. 17, Khloe Kardashan posted a funny picture of her niece North West wearing a crooked pair of sunglasses and holding up a drink with the caption, “MOOD.” 

The 2-year-old daughter of pregnant-again Kim Kardashian and Kanye West looks like she’s having a blast on vacation. And she wasn’t the only one hamming it up for Auntie KoKo’s Instagram page.
