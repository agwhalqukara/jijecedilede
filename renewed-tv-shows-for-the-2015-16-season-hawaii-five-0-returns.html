Renewed TV Shows for the 2015-16 Season: Hawaii Five-0 Returns

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, September 9, 2024

They’re back! As the end of the 2014-15 TV season approaches, networks are deciding which shows to renew — How to Get Away With Murder? duh — and which ones to axe — sorry, Mulaney, we hardly knew ye.

Among the newest batch of saved series are Hawaii Five-0 and Person of Interest. CBS announced on Monday, May 11, that both shows — as well as 13 others — will be returning for at least one more season.

Other network staples including Survivor, NCIS, Criminal Minds, and The Good Wife will also be back. The latter aired its season 6 finale on May 10, reuniting leading lady Alicia Florrick (Julianna Margulies) and her former BFF Kalinda Sharma (Archie Panjabi) for one last talked-about scene. Panjabi announced last fall that this most recently ended run would be her last.

Below, find out — network by network — which other shows have officially received orders for new seasons.
