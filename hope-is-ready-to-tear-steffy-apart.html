Hope Is Ready To Tear Steffy Apart

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, September 26, 2024

The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers reveal more angst and animosity between longtime rivals and sometimes stepsisters Hope and Steffy.

The Bold and the Beautiful Spoilers: Steffy Is Relentless

For some reason, Steffy Forrester Finnegan (Jacqueline MacInnes Wood) thinks it is perfectly within her rights to judge someone else’s relationship when she knows nothing about it and tell the two people in the relationship what she wants them to do. She even thinks it’s perfectly reasonable that they would do exactly as she says.

Well, Hope Logan (Annika Noelle) is not about to do that and can’t believe the absolute gall of Steffy to tell her she is using Thomas Forrester (Matthew Atkinson) as a sex toy and doesn’t love him. Hope insists that she does love Thomas, but Steffy doesn’t care.

Next week, Steffy won’t back down and Hope is not having it. After Steffy hurls more insights, Hope rightfully lifts up her hand to smack Steffy across the mouth like she deserves, but Steffy grabs her hand and stops her.

Hope Is Desperate, Thomas Is Lost

Hope is absolutely distraught after knowing how much she hurt Thomas and listening to Steffy chew her out for not being ready to marry again. She takes her complaints to Brooke Logan (Katherine Kelly Lang) and Ridge Forrester (Thorsten Kaye) and vows that she is not going to allow Steffy to come between her and Thomas. After all, she said no to marriage, she didn’t say no to being with Thomas.

Steffy decides to take matters into her own hands again and stops by Thomas’s place for another chat. She finds him depressed on his couch wondering what the last nine months of his life have been about. Was he fooling himself into believing Hope really loved him?

Steffy tells him yes, he was and once again orders him to banish Hope from his life forever. After all the years of waiting for Hope to tell him that she loves him, will Thomas listen to his busybody sister instead? Hopefully, Hope is able to get through to him and make him realize they don’t need to be married to be together.

Tune into The Bold and the Beautiful weekdays on CBS to see how these stories play out.
