Ashley Graham Is Pregnant with Twins!

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Ashley Graham just revealed she’s pregnant with twins and she revealed the sex of the babies on the way, too!

The 33-year-old model announced her pregnancy with husband Justin Ervin back in July. Today, she released a video of the moment she found out she’s expecting two!!!

Click inside to see…

While having her ultrasound done, the technician indicates she’s having twin boys.

“Are you serious? We’re gonna have three boys?” Ashley exclaims in the video. “You are kidding me!”

Their first child, Isaac Menelik was born on January 18, 2020.

Congratulations to the happy couple on the amazing news!
